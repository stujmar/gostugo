Timms Valley (2013)

  • The Project: Timms Valley is a stopmotion animation TV pilot for IFC.
  • My Role: I played three major roles throughout the project: breaking down animation in preproduction to fabrication rigs and solutions for each shot; fabricating props and machining puppet armatures; and during production I was the Director of Animation, managing five animation stages and a crew of 12 animators.
  • Takeaways: I learned a lot about narrative television production and even more about stopmotion animation and fabrication. Seeing the process of taking a story idea all the way to a pilot inspired me to work harder to bring my own ideas to life. Following this experience, I started my own company, Edgewater Workbench
