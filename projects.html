Take a looks at some of my projects throughout the years.

Earthmounds

  • The Project: Earthmounds were a long time coming ever since college I had been sketching hills with eyes, topped by little trees and houses and imagined sotries playing out where their problems mirrored the problems of the biomes they represented. Eventually it evolved into an education brand that focuses on connecting audiences wiht nature through being able to empathise with the environment itself.
  • Take Aways: What I learned was. I learned to source materials from china and deal with customs and frieght. I learn how to file copywrites and trademarks. I learned how to register UPCs and design PDQ/POP for store shelves. Cold calling retails store and distributors. It really gave me focus to hone my art skills to be more polished and commercially viable as well.
