Earthmounds

Earthmounds was a long time coming. Since college I have been sketching hills with eyes, topped by little trees and houses. I imagined stories playing out, where the anthropomorphic land masses' problems mirrored the problems of the biomes they represented. Over the last decade, I've dedicated more energy toward the concept and it has evolved into an educational brand. Earthmounds aims to connect audiences with nature, helping them empathize with the environment itself through cute characterizations.

Banner Ninja
